<h2 style="margin-top: 0;">GIR Qualifying Events</h2>
<p style="font-size: 1.2rem; margin-top: 1rem; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
    Federal and Maryland law create guaranteed issue rights when specific qualifying events occur:
    </p>
<div class="grid-3">
    <div class="trigger-category" style="border-top: 4px solid var(--accent-cool);">
        <h3>Loss of Coverage</h3>
        <p>Coverage ended through no fault of their own</p>
    </div>
    <div class="trigger-category" style="border-top: 4px solid var(--accent-warm);">
        <h3>Misconduct or Bankruptcy</h3>
        <p>Something went wrong with the insurance carrier</p>
    </div>
    <div class="trigger-category" style="border-top: 4px solid var(--secondary);">
        <h3>Trial Rights</h3>
        <p>Tried something new and want to return</p>
    </div>
</div>
