<h2>Insurance Carrier Requirements</h2>
<p style="margin-top: 1rem;">When a Medicare beneficiary has guaranteed issue rights, insurers are legally required to:</p>
<div style="margin-top: 1.5rem; max-width: 850px;">
<div class="trigger-item" style="background: #ffffff;">
<div class="trigger-number" style="background: var(--accent-cool);">✓</div>
<div class="trigger-text">
<strong>Accept their application</strong>
                        Cannot deny or condition issuance based on health status<sup>6</sup>
</div>
</div>
<div class="trigger-item" style="background: #ffffff;">
<div class="trigger-number" style="background: var(--accent-cool);">✓</div>
<div class="trigger-text">
<strong>Charge standard rates</strong>
                        No price discrimination based on health, claims, or conditions<sup>7</sup>
</div>
</div>
<div class="trigger-item" style="background: #ffffff;">
<div class="trigger-number" style="background: var(--accent-cool);">✓</div>
<div class="trigger-text">
<strong>Waive pre-existing exclusions</strong>
                        If they have 6+ months of <span class="tooltip" data-tip="Prior health coverage that counts toward eliminating pre-existing condition waiting periods">creditable coverage</span><sup>8</sup>
</div>
</div>
</div>
