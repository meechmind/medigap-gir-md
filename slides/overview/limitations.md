<h2>Critical Limitations</h2>
<p style="margin-bottom: 1rem;">GIRs have important boundaries:</p>
<div class="two-column">
<div>
<h3 style="color: var(--base);">GIRs Do NOT:</h3>
<ul class="not-list">
<li>Guarantee a specific premium price</li>
<li>Allow any plan you want (specific plans for specific triggers)</li>
<li>Extend indefinitely</li>
<li>Apply when you lose coverage for non-payment</li>
<li>Apply when you committed fraud</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<div class="key-stat" style="background: var(--secondary);">63 Days</div>
<p style="font-size: 1.2rem; margin-top: 0.75rem;">
<strong>Hard deadline.</strong> All GIRs have a 63-day enrollment window.<sup>9</sup>
</p>
<p style="color: var(--base); margin-top: 0.5rem;">
                        Missing this deadline eliminates the protection entirely.
                    </p>
</div>
</div>
