<h2>Maryland's Additional Protections</h2>
<div class="two-column" style="margin-top: 1.5rem;">
<div class="card" style="border-left: 4px solid var(--secondary);">
<h3>Birthday Rule</h3>
<p style="font-size: 1rem;"><strong>Effective July 1, 2023</strong></p>
<ul style="margin-top: 0.75rem;">
<li>Annual open enrollment: birthday + 30 days</li>
<li>Switch to plan of equal or lesser benefits</li>
<li>No medical underwriting required</li>
</ul>
