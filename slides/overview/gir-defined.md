<h1>What is a Guaranteed Issue Right?</h1>
<p style="font-size: 1.35rem; max-width: 850px; line-height: 1.7; margin-top: 1.5rem;">
                A legal protection requiring insurance companies to sell Medicare beneficiaries a <span class="tooltip" data-tip="Medicare Supplement Insurance that helps pay costs Original Medicare doesn't cover">Medigap</span> policy <span class="emphasis">regardless of health status or pre-existing conditions</span>.<sup>1</sup>
</p>
<div style="margin-top: 1rem; max-width: 850px;">
<div>
<h3 style="color: var(--accent-cool);">What It Means</h3>
<ul class="check-list">
<li>No <span class="tooltip" data-tip="The process during which insurance carriers use the health information they collect from an applicant to decide whether to accept their application or to charge a higher premium.">medical underwriting</span></li>
<li>No higher charges for conditions</li>
<li>No pre-existing exclusions</li>
<li>Cannot deny your application</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="highlight-box" style="margin-top: 2rem; border-color: var(--accent-cool);">
<p>GIRs protect Medicare beneficiaries during <em>vulnerable transition periods</em> when they lose other coverage.</p>
</div>
