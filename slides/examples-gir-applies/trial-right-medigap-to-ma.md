    <h3>William, 70</h3>
    <p>Had a Medigap Plan G for 3 years. He decided to try Medicare Advantage during the Annual Election Period. After 6 months, he realizes he prefers Original Medicare.</p>
    <h3 style="margin-top: 1rem;">Result</h3>
    <p>William should first contact his original Medigap company. If Plan G is still available, he can return to it. If not, he can purchase Plans A, B, C, F, K, or L from any issuer.<sup>22</sup></p>
    <p class="citation">Trigger: Had Medigap, tried MA for first time, switching back within 12 months</p>
