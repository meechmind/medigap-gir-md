<h2>Example: Medigap Policyholder Tries MA</h2>

<h3>William, 70</h3>
<p>Had a Medigap Plan G for 3 years. Decided to try Medicare Advantage during Annual Election Period. After 6 months, realizes he prefers Original Medicare.</p>
<h3 style="margin-top: 1rem;">Result</h3>
<p>William should first contact his original Medigap company. If Plan G is still available, he can return to it. If not, he can purchase Plans A, B, C, F, K, or L from any issuer.<sup>22</sup></p>
<p class="citation">Trigger 6: Had Medigap, tried MA for first time, within 12 months</p>
