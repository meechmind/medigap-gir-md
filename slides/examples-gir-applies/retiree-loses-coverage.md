<h2>Example: Retiree Loses Employer Coverage</h2>
<div class="scenario-box positive">
<div class="scenario-label positive">âœ“ GIR Applies</div>
<h3>Margaret, 72</h3>
<p>Has had retiree health coverage from former employer for 7 years. In March, she receives notice the company is eliminating retiree health benefits effective June 1.</p>
<h3 style="margin-top: 1rem;">Result</h3>
<p>Margaret has GIR and can enroll in Plans A, B, C, F, K, or L without medical underwriting through approximately August 3 (63 days after June 1).<sup>19</sup></p>
<p class="citation">Trigger 1: Employer-sponsored supplemental coverage terminates</p>
