<h2>Five Key Points</h2>
<div class="card" style="display: flex; gap: 2rem; margin-bottom: 1rem">
    <div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--secondary); ">1</div>
    <div>
        <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem;">Time-Limited</h3>
        <p style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-bottom: 0">GIR periods generally last 63 days. Missing this window eliminates protection entirely.</p>
    </div>
</div>
<div class="card" style="display: flex; gap: 2rem; margin-bottom: 1rem">
    <div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--accent-cool);">2</div>
        <div>
            <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem;">Documentation Required</h3>
            <p style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-bottom: 0">Submit evidence of triggering event. Keep all termination notices and disenrollment confirmations.</p>
        </div>
</div>
<div class="card" style="display: flex; gap: 2rem; margin-bottom: 1rem">
    <div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--accent-warm);">3</div>
    <div>
        <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem;">Plans Vary by Trigger</h3>
        <p style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-bottom: 0">Not all triggers give access to all plans. Know which plans are available for the specific situation.</p>
    </div>
</div>
<div class="card" style="display: flex; gap: 2rem; margin-bottom: 1rem">
    <div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--primary);">4</div>
    <div>
        <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem;">12-Month Trial Window</h3>
        <p style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-bottom: 0">If trying Medicare Advantage for the first time, there is a one-year window to decide to return to Original Medicare with Medigap protection.</p>
    </div>
</div>
<div class="card" style="display: flex; gap: 2rem; margin-bottom: 1rem">
    <div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--base);">5</div>
    <div>
        <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem;">Non-Payment and Dissatisfaction Don't Qualify</h3>
        <p style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-bottom: 0">Losing coverage due to missed premiums or dissatisfaction with terms do not create a GIR.</p>
    </div>
</div>
