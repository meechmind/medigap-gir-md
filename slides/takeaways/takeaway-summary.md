<h2>Five Essential Points</h2>
<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(2, 1fr); gap: 1rem; margin-top: 1rem;">
<div class="card">
<div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--secondary);">1</div>
<h3 style="font-size: 1rem;">Time-Limited</h3>
<p style="font-size: 0.9rem;">All GIR periods last exactly 63 days. Missing this window eliminates protection entirely.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--accent-cool);">2</div>
<h3 style="font-size: 1rem;">Documentation Required</h3>
<p style="font-size: 0.9rem;">Submit evidence of triggering event. Keep all termination notices and disenrollment confirmations.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--accent-warm);">3</div>
<h3 style="font-size: 1rem;">Plans Vary by Trigger</h3>
<p style="font-size: 0.9rem;">Not all triggers give access to all plans. Know which plans are available for your specific situation.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--primary);">4</div>
<h3 style="font-size: 1rem;">12-Month Trial Window</h3>
<p style="font-size: 0.9rem;">If you try Medicare Advantage, you have one year to decide if you want to return with Medigap protection.</p>
</div>
<div class="card" style="grid-column: span 2;">
<div class="icon-number" style="background: var(--base);">5</div>
<h3 style="font-size: 1rem;">Non-Payment and Fraud Don't Qualify</h3>
<p style="font-size: 0.9rem;">Losing coverage because you didn't pay premiums or committed fraud does not create a GIR.</p>
</div>
</div>
